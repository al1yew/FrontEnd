PASHA Group In Brief

“PASHA Holding” Limited Liability Company (PASHA Holding or PASHA Holding LLC) through its subsidiaries, invests in banking, insurance, construction, information technology, the property development and tourism sectors.

Established in 2006, the Holding builds its business on the basis of effective management, corporate governance and well-thought-out investment decisions.

The Holding has two types of investment assets portfolios: Controlling stakes in PASHA Holding and its subsidiaries (PASHA Group) and minority private equity investments.
